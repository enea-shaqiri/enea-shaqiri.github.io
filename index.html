About Me

Hi Internaut, my name is Enea! I have a MsC in pure Mathematics, and I am currently hunting for patterns and inefficiencies in the markets. I really enjoy topics that live in the intersection between Math and the real world. Many topics can be found in this intersection and I have had the pleasure to explore some of them. My current focus is on market microstructure and market making models. I find really cool that it is not too difficult to code a bot implementing strategies based on these models and have it trade live on the markets! I also enjoy warping my head around how to build complex strategies mixing esoteric derivatives like everlasting options and Uniswap V3.


About this blog

Learning new stuff is always fun! I love that revelation moment when all the pieces of the puzzle fit together and you get a view of the full picture. The purpose of this blog is to structure my thoughts/notes about things I deem interesting and avoid forgetting the steps I have taken to understand them. You might find Math, Deep Learning, financial models, or a combination of the above! Stay tuned!
